# Shibei — brand & vision

## Name
**Shibei On-Chain Agent** · 拾贝链上交易 Agent
*拾贝 (shí bèi) — "gathering shells," picking up value from the tides.*

## Tagline
**Your keys. Your strategy. An autonomous agent that trades on-chain so you don't have to.**

(shorter: *Self-custody AI trading, autonomous on BNB Chain.*)

## Vision
Shibei is an **autonomous, self-custody trading agent on BNB Chain**. It reads the
market through **CoinMarketCap's Agent Hub**, decides with a disciplined
**multi-layer risk engine**, and executes every trade on-chain via the **Trust
Wallet Agent Kit** — your private key signs locally and never leaves your device.

No custodial transfer. No black box. No human in the loop — just a tireless agent
whose every decision is auditable and whose risk is always under control.

We're building toward a world where anyone can deploy **institutional-grade,
on-chain trading agents they fully own and can trust** — because you never give up
your keys.

### Three pillars
1. **Self-custody by design** — your key signs every trade locally (EIP-712); funds never move custodially.
2. **Discipline over hype** — a real multi-layer risk engine (sizing, breakeven, circuit breakers, drawdown kill-switches), not a price-feed-wired-to-a-swap toy.
3. **Fully on-chain & autonomous** — it reads, decides, executes, and manages positions 24/7 on BNB Chain.

## Logo
A golden **scallop shell** (拾贝) whose ribs fan upward like a **rising chart** —
value gathered from the market — anchored by a single **node at its base**: the
self-custody agent that signs locally. BNB-gold on deep chain-night.
